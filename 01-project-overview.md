# Project Overview

## Problem

Small and local sellers often depend on scattered channels: social media for discovery, chat apps for conversations, separate payment links for checkout, and manual order tracking. Buyers also struggle to discover trustworthy nearby products, compare sellers, save items, and complete purchases in one smooth journey.

## Solution

Vyom brings the full local commerce journey into one mobile app:

- Buyers discover products through home recommendations, feeds, reels, stories, and live sessions.
- Sellers promote products through posts, stories, livestreams, and seller profiles.
- Buyers and sellers communicate through realtime inbox conversations.
- Buyers complete orders through cart, checkout, Razorpay payment, and order tracking.
- Sellers manage products, orders, wallet actions, reviews, and notifications from a seller dashboard.
- AI embeddings support smarter product search and recommendation behavior.

## Target Users

- Buyers looking for local, trending, and personalized products.
- Sellers who want a mobile storefront without building their own app.
- Small businesses that need simple product, order, customer, and content management.
- Judges and evaluators looking for a scalable full-stack mobile commerce prototype.

## Core Capabilities

- Account registration, login, email verification, password reset, and Google sign-in.
- Product catalog with featured, trending, local, and personalized sections.
- Product details with seller info, reviews, delivery estimate, wishlist, and similar products.
- Social feed with posts, reels, likes, saves, follows, and product tagging.
- Stories with media, reactions, viewers, and 24-hour expiry behavior.
- Livestream shopping with Agora video, live chat, reactions, pinned products, Q&A, and live orders.
- Cart, checkout summary, Razorpay payment, order verification, cancellation, reorder, and ratings.
- Wishlist with collections.
- Buyer account dashboard, addresses, profile, security, seller onboarding, and data export.
- Seller dashboard for analytics, products, orders, wallet withdrawals, reviews, and push tokens.
- Inbox with realtime chat, typing state, read status, alerts, order updates, and reactions.

## Product Differentiators

- Combines commerce and creator-style content in a single mobile experience.
- Supports both buyer and seller workflows.
- Includes realtime livestream commerce, not only static product listings.
- Uses location and AI embeddings to improve product discovery.
- Provides a clear path from discovery to payment to seller fulfillment.
